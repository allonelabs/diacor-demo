Our History

2009

Founding of the Clinic

The DIACOR clinic was founded for the comprehensive management of non-communicable, chronic, and comorbid diseases. With the support of the Japanese government, a diabetes prevention program was implemented, examining 2,800 patients.

2010

International Recognition

Research results were published in high-impact journals and presented at 4 international congresses: EASD (Vienna), IDF (Montreal), WEC (Kyoto), WCDP (Dresden). The project was recognized by the Japanese government as the most successful project.

2018

IDF Certification

DIACOR has been awarded the International Diabetes Federation (IDF) "Center of Education" (Center of Education) and "Center of Excellence" (Center of Excellence) status.
