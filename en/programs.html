Diabetes Checkup

Full diabetes diagnostics, monitoring and prevention. Ideal for patients with diabetes or at-risk groups.

  • Endocrinologist consultation
  • HbA1c (glycated hemoglobin)
  • Fasting glucose
  • Lipid profile
  • Kidney function (creatinine, urea)
  • Liver function test
  • Ophthalmological screening (diabetic retinopathy)

Cardio-Pulmonary Checkup

Comprehensive cardiopulmonary assessment with modern equipment. Recommended for patients aged 40+.

  • Cardiologist consultation
  • Pulmonologist consultation

  • 24-hr blood pressure monitoring
  • Lipid profile
  • Complete blood count
